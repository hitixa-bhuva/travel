Redefining the Way You Travel

A legacy of trust, service, and excellence.

Founded by Mr. Pravin Chaudhari and unified under the leadership of Mr. Darvin Chaudhari, DC Group was officially established on 1st August 2025 as the umbrella brand for four specialized travel companies. What began as a single travel office has now evolved into a diversified enterprise, redefining the way people experience travel across the globe.

Today, DC Group serves thousands of travelers, families, agents, and corporations worldwide, delivering seamless travel planning, curated holiday experiences, expert solutions, and comprehensive insurance coverage. With a strong foundation of trust, service, and excellence, we continue to bring the world closer to you—one journey at a time.

Four Companies, One Unified Vision

Specialized expertise, united by trust.

DC Tours & Travels

As the first venture of DC Group, DC Tours & Travels has been simplifying travel since 2012. Whether it’s domestic trips, international adventures, or family vacations, we handle the planning so you can focus on the memories.

DC Holidays

Launched in 2024, DC Holidays specializes in stress-free holiday packages that combine comfort, fun, and adventure. Whether you’re exploring India’s treasures or discovering global wonders, we make sure your vacation is more than just a trip — it’s a memory.

DC The Travel Expert

Founded in 2022, DC The Travel Expert was built to serve travelers who want more than the ordinary. From corporate MICE services to adventure treks and tailor-made trips, our expertise ensures that your travel perfectly aligns with your goals and passions.

DC Insurance Solution

Since 2014, DC Insurance Solution has been the safety backbone of DC Group. Travel comes with unexpected risks — and we make sure you’re prepared for them. With our reliable coverage, you can explore the world knowing your health, time, and money are protected.
