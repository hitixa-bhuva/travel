About Us – DC Group

Founded on Vision. United by Legacy. Built with Love.

DC Group, officially established on 1st August 2025, is more than just a brand—it’s the union of passion, persistence, and purpose. What started in 2012, when Mr. Pravin Chaudhari opened a modest travel office with the dream of making journeys simple and memorable, has grown into a diversified group of trusted companies.

In 2025, his son Mr. Darvin Chaudhari carried this vision forward by uniting them under one powerful identity — DC Group. Today, the group proudly serves 50,000+ travelers, agents, and corporations across India and beyond, transforming a small-town dream into a global legacy of trust.

Who We Are

Guiding Principles That Drive Everything We Do

DC Group is more than a travel company — it is a legacy built on trust, values, and innovation. Officially formed in 2025, the group stands as a union of services designed to make travel easier, safer, and more meaningful.

Our Commitment / Promise to You

Values That Unite Our People And Guide Our Purpose.

At DC Group, our commitment goes beyond travel. Every journey we design is built on love, integrity, and trust. We believe travel isn’t just about destinations—it’s about the memories you create, the bonds you strengthen, and the stories you carry for a lifetime. With over 12 years of experience and the blessing of our founder’s vision, we promise to serve every traveler not as a client, but as family. Our promise is simple: to make every journey seamless, safe, and unforgettable.
